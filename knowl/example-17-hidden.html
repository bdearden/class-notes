Consider the following argument:

  • If your inbox is full, then you can't receive emails.
  • Your inbox is full.
  • Therefore you can't receive emails.

This argument is of the form:

  • \(p \to q\)
  • \(p\)
  • \(\therefore q\)

and is valid since \(((p \to q) \wedge p) \to q\) is a tautology.
