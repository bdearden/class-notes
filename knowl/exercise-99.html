Exercise 1.

Let \(A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 4 \\ -3 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix}\) and let \(B = \begin{bmatrix} 5 & 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\text{,}\) find:

  1. \(A + B\)
  2. \(B - A\)
  3. \(AB\)
  4. \(A^t\)
  5. \(B^t\)
  6. \(B^t A^t\)
  7. \((AB)^t\)

You can check your work with Sage. If you want to find the transpose of a matrix named \(A\text{,}\) you type A.transpose(). But remember, you can't use the computer or a fancy calculator on the test.
