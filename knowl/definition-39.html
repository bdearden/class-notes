Definition 2.3.7.

The identity matrix of order \(n\) is the \(n \times n\) matrix:

\begin{equation*} I_n = \begin{bmatrix} \delta_{ij} \end{bmatrix} \text{ where } \delta_{ij} = \left\{ \begin{array}{lr} 1 & i = j \\ 0 & i \not= j \end{array} \right. \end{equation*}

I'll refer to this as the \(n\times n\) identity matrix.
